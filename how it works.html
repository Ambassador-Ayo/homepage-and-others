How It Works

For Domestic Helpers:

Step 1: Sign Up and Create a Profile
Visit the SwiftHire website or dial *943* and sign up as a domestic helper.
Fill out your profile with accurate information, including your skills, qualifications, work experience, and a clear profile picture.

Step 2: Verification and Background Check
SwiftHire will conduct a thorough background check and verification process to ensure your trustworthiness and reliability. Once verified, your profile will be marked as trustworthy.

Step 3: Browse and Apply for Jobs
Explore available job listings in your area.
Review job details, requirements, and employer profiles.
Apply to the jobs that match your skills and availability.

Step 4: Communicate with Employers
Once an employer receives your application, they may contact you through the platform's messaging system.
Engage in conversations with potential employers to discuss job details, schedules, and expectations.

Step 5: Secure a Job
If both you and the employer are satisfied with the terms and conditions, you can accept the job offer.
SwiftHire will facilitate the agreement between you and the employer.

Step 6: Perform the Job
Arrive at the job location as agreed upon and perform your duties to the best of your abilities.
Maintain open communication with the employer to ensure a smooth working relationship.

Step 7: Get Paid Securely
After completing the job, the employer will make payment through SwiftHire's secure payment system.
You will receive your payment promptly and securely.

Step 8: Leave a Review
After completing a job, both you and the employer have the option to leave a review for each other.
Share your feedback, rating, and comments to help others in the SwiftHire community make informed choices.

For Employers:

Step 1: Sign Up and Create a Profile
Sign up on the SwiftHire platform as an employer.
Complete your profile with accurate information, including your location and specific domestic service needs.

Step 2: Browse Profiles and Post Jobs
Explore profiles of qualified domestic helpers in your area.
Review their skills, qualifications, and user reviews.
If you don't find a suitable match, you can post a job listing with your requirements.

Step 3: Connect with Domestic Helpers
When you find a potential match, contact the domestic helper directly through SwiftHire's messaging system.
Discuss job details, schedules, rates, and any specific requirements.

Step 4: Review Applicants and Hire
Receive and review applications from domestic helpers.
Conduct interviews or additional screening if desired.
Choose the domestic helper who best meets your needs and hire them.

Step 5: Confirm the Job
Finalize the terms and conditions of the job with the chosen domestic helper.
SwiftHire will help facilitate the agreement to ensure clarity and transparency.

Step 6: Enjoy Hassle-Free Help
Once everything is in order, the domestic helper will start providing the agreed-upon services.
Enjoy the convenience of reliable domestic help.

Step 7: Secure Payment
Pay the domestic helper securely through SwiftHire's payment system after the job is completed to your satisfaction.
SwiftHire ensures a smooth and secure payment process for both parties.
SwiftHire aims to make the process of finding domestic help or hiring domestic workers straightforward, secure, and efficient for all users.

Step 8: Leave a Review
After the job is completed, share your feedback, rating, and comments to help others in the SwiftHire community make informed choices.
